About IIIT Tiruchirappalli

The Indian Institute of Information Technology, Tiruchirappalli (IIIT Trichy) is a premier institution of higher learning, dedicated to imparting quality education in the fields of Information Technology and related disciplines. Established in 2013, IIIT Trichy has grown to become a hub of academic excellence, innovation, and research.

Our Mission:

Our mission is to provide a world-class education that empowers students to become leaders in their chosen fields, and to contribute to the betterment of society through innovation, entrepreneurship, and social responsibility.

Our Vission:

Our vision is to become a globally recognized institution, renowned for its academic excellence, innovative research, and socially responsible graduates.

Convocation Objectives:

  • To confer degrees upon our graduating students
  • To recognize and honor outstanding academic achievements
  • To provide a platform for students to showcase their projects and innovations
  • To foster a sense of community and camaraderie among students, faculty, and alumni
  • To inspire and motivate our students to become responsible citizens and leaders
